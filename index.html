How to Play Blackjack

The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

Card Values

  1. Face cards are worth 10
  2. Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever makes a better hand
  3. Numerical value of a card is its value

How to play

  1. Press Hit to deal another card
  2. Press Stay to hold your current total and end your turn.
  3. If you go over 21 you bust
